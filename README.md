## What is Praba?

**Praba (Personal Responsive AI-Based Assistant)** is an offline-first AI assistant built for students and users who need AI access anywhere — even without internet.

It runs an AI language model directly on your device. No data is sent to any server. No subscription. No sign up. Just open and chat.

---

## Features

- 🔌 **100% Offline** — AI runs entirely on your device after first setup
- 🔒 **Private** — your conversations never leave your phone
- 💬 **Streaming responses** — replies appear token by token
- 🧠 **Model switcher** — switch between Praba model versions
- 📂 **Chat history** — all conversations saved locally
- 📲 **Installable** — add to iPhone home screen, works like a native app
