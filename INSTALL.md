# Installing Praba on iPhone

Praba is a Progressive Web App — no App Store needed.
You install it directly from Safari in a few taps.

---

## What You Need

- iPhone with iOS 16.4 or later
- Safari browser
- WiFi for the first setup (around 600MB download)
- Around 700MB of free storage on your iPhone

---

## Step 1 — Open Safari

Open **Safari** on your iPhone.

You must use Safari — Chrome, Firefox and other browsers on iPhone do not support PWA installation.

---

## Step 2 — Go to the Praba link

Type this in the Safari address bar:

```
https://prabodh-dev.github.io/praba
```

Wait for the page to load fully.

---

## Step 3 — Add to Home Screen

1. Tap the **Share** button at the bottom of Safari
2. Scroll down in the menu that appears
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add** in the top right corner

The Praba icon will now appear on your iPhone home screen.

---

## Step 4 — First Launch

1. Tap the Praba icon on your home screen
2. You will see a loading screen — Praba is downloading the AI model
3. This is around 600MB so make sure you are on WiFi
4. Do not close the app while it is downloading
5. Once done it will go to the chat screen automatically

This download only happens once. After that Praba works fully offline.

---

## Step 5 — Use Offline

After the first setup you can turn off WiFi and mobile data.
Praba will work completely without internet.

---

## Tips

- First message after opening may take a few seconds — the model needs to warm up
- If the download fails just reopen the app and it will resume
- Praba uses around 700MB of storage for the AI model

---

## Uninstalling

1. Long press the Praba icon on your home screen
2. Tap **Remove App**
3. Tap **Delete App**

This removes Praba and frees up all storage used by the model.
